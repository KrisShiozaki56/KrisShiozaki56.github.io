Music Hear

Your inexpensive solution to ear training preparation for RCM exams!

Overview
'MUSIC HEAR!' is an Android application (currently a work in progress) designed to assist RCM examinees, specifically violin and piano learners, in preparing for the ear training components of their practical RCM exams.

Skills include: intervals, cadences, chords, sight clapping melody/rhythm clapbacks and melody playbacks.

Check out our Figma prototype, which will walk you through the app!



Team Size
2 [1 Dev, 1 Design]


Role
Designer


Timeline
May 2018 - August 2018


Tools
Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator

BACKGROUND

The inspiration for 'MUSIC HEAR!' was violin - specifically when my friends and I were preparing for our grade 10 RCM practical examinations that summer. We all noticed that we had so many resources ranging from CDs to multiple excercise books, all of which were not cheap & covered different parts (intervals, cadences, etc.) of our exams' Ear Training component.

Problem
Numerous, expensive training resources


Solution
cost-effective and cheaper solution for examinees to use to train their ears!


PLANNING & WIREFRAMES

With the goal of making a cheaper & more convenient solution, I started my empathize & research phase!

After discussing my plans with various musicians of different levels, I investigated & defined my problem and wrote down some preliminary notes to design my solution.

I then sketched the layouts of my app to get a quick visual of my idea. I sketched 18 frames after tracing an iPod touch to get a sense of the size of a phone screen! Take a look below!
