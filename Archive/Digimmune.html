Digimmune

Keeping your immunization records safe!

Overview
Digimmune is an Android application & web application, which allows you to keep a digital copy of multiple immunization records, by taking a picture of the cards

This user-friendly digital vaccine tracker was a 24hr hackathon project and my first web development project!

Check out our Devpost submission to learn about our experience at Deltahacks V and our InVision prototype, which will walk you through our mobile app!



Team Size
3 [2 Devs, 1 Design]


Role
Web Developer & Designer


Timeline
January 2019


Tools
Figma, InVision, Photoshop, Illustrator

BACKGROUND

Problem
It is easy to lose small, yellow immunization tracker cards


Solution
A safe and convenient place to store your vaccination and health card infor that can be accessed through a web application databse by doctors & clinics as well as by consumers through a mobile application


Planning & Wireframes

Before we started working, I sketched some UI ideas for the mobile app for our consumers and discussed what would be feasible and improvements that may be made later.
