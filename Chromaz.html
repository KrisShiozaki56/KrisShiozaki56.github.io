Chromaz

Games for Bored Designers

Overview
Just a simple colour game for designers to play during breaks. There are 2 games - Odd One Out and Name It. This is actually a project I'm doing with my brother and development is in progress!

Odd One Out : Find the hexagon that's a different shade from the rest. It may seem easy at first but later, the grid gets bigger and the differences are hard to see!

Name It : Did you know Imperial was a colour? How about Pewter? Here's your chance to learn all 180 colour shades and their hex colour codes in a multiple choice-styled game! You win if you identify all 180 shades correctly!
