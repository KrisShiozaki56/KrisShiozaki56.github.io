Drifts

"Don't think outside of the box. Think as if there is no box."

Thanks for visiting Drifts!

Drifts are often described as a "deviation from an expected/intended course." I named this section 'drifts' to share how I have taught myself different art types through tutorials and then added a personal twist to the final product.

Deviation is a concept and skill that I believe any individual should strive to improve, especially if you want to design/build for change.

QUOTE GRAPHICS

For as long as I can remember, I've been a huge anime & manga fan. However, it was only recently that I started to try to understand how a 'simple' comic with a few words inside a text bubble could move the audience. How could a simple phrase draw out these emotions?

I created @animabstract.quotes with the purpose of communicating how certain phrases made me feel, through abstract means.
