Drifts

"Don't think outside of the box. Think as if there is no box."

Drifts are often described as a "deviation from an expected/intended course." I named this section 'drifts' to share how I have taught myself different art types through tutorials and then added a personal twist to the final product.






CAD Projects

Before I started designing apps, I loved to make CAD creations!


Busk Shelter

While walking through the busy streets of urban cities, busking is a common sight, however sometimes the sound does not project well throughout the area and with larger crowds around you, you will not notice them.

The Busk Shelter's purpose is to provide cover and sound projection for street musicians.

Ring-Aid

Children with DCD have tight grips which cause stress and hurt muscles. This is why activities that require the use of our motor skills, such as writing, are quite difficult for those with DCD.

The goal for my personal project was to design a writing utensil that will make writing more comfortable for children with developmental coordination disorder (DCD), specifically my cousin.

Hydro Wheel

Every day, many students in Africa walk over 10km while carrying barrels of water that weigh over 40lbs. Due to this lengthy wanter collection process, some students are unable to attend school.

The Hydro Wheel's purpose is to offer an efficient and faster mode of transportation for students living in rural countries.

Ergo-Rest

As a violin player myself, I found it difficult to practice for long periods of time due to pain in my left shoulder from the pressure the shoulder rest put on it. This ergonomic violin shoulder rest reduces tension in the upper and middle trapezius muscles in the left shoulder while playing. I used electromyography to analyze the effectiveness of the ergonomic implementations and additions in my design."
